The **Account Settings** section in **Bizmo.Chat** allows users to manage their team's account details, including the team name and unique identifier. These settings ensure proper identification and accessibility of the team's workspace within the platform.

***

## **Account Settings Sections**

The **Account** settings page consists of two main sections:

### **1. Team Info**

This section allows users to update the team's name.

* **Field:** *Team Name*

* **Description:** The name of your team, which is unique to your workspace.

* **Action:**

  1. Enter the desired team name in the text input field.

  2. Click **Save** to apply changes.

### **2. Team Identifier**

This section allows users to manage the unique identifier for their team.

* **Field:** *Identifier*

* **Description:** The teamâ€™s unique identifier, used to create a custom URL for accessing the workspace. The URL format is:https://app.bizmo.chat/\{identifier}

* **Action:**

  1. Modify the identifier as needed (must be unique).

  2. Click **Save** to update the identifier.
